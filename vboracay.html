Boracay: Paradise Island

Boracay is undoubtedly the most visited among the tourist spots in Visayas. This is the favorite hangout of famous celebrities and tourists. Boasting itâ€™s white sand beach and extraordinary nightlife, Boracay really got the attention of many. Boracay is located in Malay, Aklan, Visayas, Philippines.

Boracay is renowned to the world as the perfect tourist spot for relaxation. It is a small island with an area of 3.98 square miles. Many awards Boracay had received from local to international. In fact, it has been awarded as the best island in the world by travel magazine Travel + Leisure in 2012.

The White Beach raised the name of Boracay to many counties. During the months of November to May, tourists around the world gather in Boracay because it is during these months that the beach is calm due to Amihan Season.

On the other side of the Island, Bulabog Beach can be found where the waves are high and the winds blow wild. This is the perfect spot for windsurfing and kiteboarding.
